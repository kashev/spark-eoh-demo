About HKN

HKN (or Eta Kappa Nu) is the honors society for Electrical and Computer Engineering. It is a subsidiary of IEEE, and was founded here at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.

We are very excited to be here at EOH 2015, and hope our exhibits give you a better idea of what it is to be an Electrical and Computer Engineer.
